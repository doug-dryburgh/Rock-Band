Music

I Love Maple Syrup

This bangin anthem was first released on Rock Band's debut album Rock Your Face Off. It held the Bilboard number one place for an astonishing 342 weeks.

Poutine is Delicious

Inspired by a late night visit to a 24 hour poutine stand this song expresses what every Canadian feels. The song found surprising success in Iceland where the locals do not know what poutine is, but are non the less very enthusiastic about the guitar riffs.

The Beaver is a Noble Animal

Considered by many to be the unofficial second national anthem of Canada. This song pays homage to the greatest of animals: the beaver.
